# Product

## Register

product

## Users

CareerHub serves individual job seekers managing applications, offers, interviews, availability, and career decisions. Users are often comparing incomplete or uncertain information and need dense details to remain understandable at a glance.

## Product Purpose

CareerHub helps an individual manage the full job-search lifecycle and make grounded career decisions. Success means the product presents compensation, trade-offs, progress, and next actions clearly enough that users can trust the result and understand the assumptions behind it.

## Brand Personality

Refined, clear, trustworthy, and practical. The product should feel considered and high quality without becoming decorative or theatrical.

## Anti-references

- Flashy fintech styling that makes financial data feel promotional.
- Decorative effects that compete with the user's task.
- Overly sparse layouts that hide important assumptions or reduce comparability.
- Opaque scoring that presents uncertain inputs as guaranteed value.

## Design Principles

- Show the assumptions behind calculated values.
- Distinguish confirmed value from uncertain or non-liquid value.
- Keep dense workflows scannable through hierarchy and consistent patterns.
- Prefer familiar controls and precise language over novelty.
- Use visual polish to improve comprehension, not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain sufficient text and control contrast, full keyboard access, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, and status communication that does not rely on color alone.
